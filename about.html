About Us

Empowering the GTA V modding community with innovative tools

Our Story

Script Hook V .NET was born from a passion for gaming and software development. We recognized the need for a robust, modern framework that would enable developers to create sophisticated modifications for Grand Theft Auto V using the power and flexibility of the .NET Framework.

What started as a personal project has grown into one of the most trusted and widely-used modding frameworks in the GTA V community. Our commitment to quality, performance, and developer experience has made us the go-to choice for both hobbyist modders and professional developers.

Our Mission

To provide the most powerful, reliable, and accessible modding framework for GTA V, enabling developers of all skill levels to bring their creative visions to life while maintaining the highest standards of performance and stability.
